Privacy policy

We, at Throw It, respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may provide us with. This Privacy Policy outlines the type of information we may collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to safeguard your personal information.

Collection of Information

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and contact number when you place an order for our garbage collection services. We also collect non-personal information such as your browser type, IP address, and the pages you visited on our website.

Use of Information

We may use your personal information to provide you with the services you have requested, process payments, and communicate with you about our services. We may also use non-personal information to improve our website's performance and user experience.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless required by law or for the purpose of providing our services to you. We may share non-personal information with third-party service providers to analyze website performance and user behavior.

Security of Information

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We use secure technologies and follow industry-standard practices to safeguard your information.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on our website, and your continued use of our services will signify your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support page.
